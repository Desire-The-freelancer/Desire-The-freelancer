WHAT IS COVID-19?

Covid-19 is a global pandemic that has affected the whole world. The first case of covid 19 was detected in December 2019. It was taken as a light thing by that time because the were no death cases, Time flied to March 2020 and a lot of people had already died of Covid-19 at that time. The whole world had to stop everything in the process of lockdown. South africa had its first Covid-19 lockdown in March 2020. As we are talking today, South Africa has recorded 70 000 deaths related to Covid-19. The are now 14 000 number of active cases related to Covid-19 and there are 3 000 people in the whole South Africa who are in a quarantine. Individuals are calculated to have a lifespan of 2 weeks to be alive until they die of Covid-19 if the virus is not treated.
