Akira Toriyama

The man who inspired and entertained milloins

The timeline of Akira Toriyama

  • 1955 - born in Kiyosu, Aichi, Japan.
  • 1978 – 1983 - He quit his day job, and started creating manga and anime. He went on to create Dr. Slump which was his first success, which got him Shogakukan Manga Award for best shōnen or shōjo manga series of the year.
  • 1983 – 1997 - Inspired by kung-fu movies, He made dragon-boy, which was loved and evolved to the famous dragon ball. Dragon ball became one of the best selling manga of all time, which later on got multiple video games and animated Series (dragon ball z) and movies (dragon ball z)
  • 1996 - Made Dragon ball GT.
  • 2005 - CQ Motors began selling an electric car designed by Toriyama.
  • 2006 - Together with the maker of One piece they created Cress Epoch, the cross over between Dragon ball z and One piece.
  • 2009 - He was the creative consultant of the American live adaptation of dragon ball Z, which failed badly.
  • 2012 - After 17 years dragon ball z made its return with Dragon ball z:battle of the Gods movie that Toriyama was involved in. Then later was the one who was designing the characters for Dragon ball super which was made later on after the dragon ball z movies.
  • 2015 - He was involved in the creation of the Dragon ball z: Ressurection F film.
  • 2016 - Thats when Dragon ball super was aired and was a great success
  • 2018 - He created the Dragon ball super:Broly movie.
  • 2022 - He created the Dragon ball super:Superhero movie.
  • 2024 - before his death he finished the story and character design for the upcoming dragon ball Daima anime Series.
  • 2024 - He passed away at 68.

"The role of my manga is to be a work of entertainment through and through. I dare say I don't care even if [my works] have left nothing behind, as long as they have entertained their readers."

—Akira Toriyama, 2013
